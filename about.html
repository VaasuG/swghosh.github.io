About Us

Meet our Technology and Resource partner Cialfo, a brainchild of an accomplished Martinian Rohan Pasari :)

Mr Rohan Pasari is the Co founder and CEO of Cialfo. He started Cialfo in 2012 with the belief that students’ focus on grades needs to shift towards “learning to learn”. Cialfo makes the application process for

higher education simple, efficient and accessible to all. Cialfo was among the top 20 startups in Singapore 2014 and is an alumni of Blackbox, a Silicon Valley based accelerator. Cialfo has since been featured in

Wall Street Journal, Straits Times, MyPaper and Singapore Business Review. Mr Rohan Pasari is a double Gold Medalist from NTU with a background in Engineering, Economics and Entrepreneurship. Mr Pasari is also a member

of International Association for College Admission Counseling (IACAC) and has been invited to speaks at various conferences such as Echelon, EdTech summit.

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oA8YSFoF9a4

Here is what Mr. Pasari has to say about Mrs Sucharita Basu, our Director :

"Ma'am Basu was my History teacher and also the Class teacher in grade 9-10, but her impact in my life (and I can vouch the same for my friends in my class as well) went far beyond just understanding History. She played an

integral part in laying the foundation of the building blocks of our personality and value system. It takes a village to raise a child and outside the comfort of our homes, we had our school and in it Ma'am Basu, one

of the most prominent pillars. I am forever grateful."
